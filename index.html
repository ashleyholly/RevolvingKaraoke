Revolving Karaoke

Dine. Dance. Sing.

Revolving Karaoke is a a tech interactive Japanese restaurant located in Downtown SLO which features a unique dining experience of a rotating sushi bar where each customer can pick out their sushi off of the bar. Revolving Karaoke also includes a karaoke bar where customers can sing along to their favorite throwbacks.
